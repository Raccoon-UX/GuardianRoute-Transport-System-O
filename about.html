GuardianRoute

Our Vision & Mission

Making every school journey safe, smart, and sustainable for Mira Bhayandar.

The Problem We Solve

Many school transportation systems in Mira Bhayandar operate without real-time tracking, leaving parents anxious and routes inefficient. This leads to higher fuel consumption and traffic congestion near our schools.

GuardianRoute bridges this gap with an intelligent tracking solution that ensures student safety and provides total peace of mind without requiring expensive hardware.
